2014 - Mazda6 - HVAC

REFRIGERANT LINE

Purpose

  • The system piping connects each related part of the refrigerant system.

Construction

  • Block-type piping has been adopted to the pipe connection, improving the efficiency of piping related servicing.

  • The pipes in the piping system consist of the following parts.

    • Cooler hose (HI)

    • Cooler hose (LO)

    • Cooler pipe

  • The pipes in the piping system are made of aluminum alloy and the hoses are made of rubber (flexible hose).

  • A high-pressure charging valve is located on the cooler pipe (HI) and a low-pressure charging valve is located on the cooler pipe (LO).

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
