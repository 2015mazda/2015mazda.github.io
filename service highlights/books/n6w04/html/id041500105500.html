DSC OFF SWITCH, DSC OFF INDICATOR LIGHT

Purpose/Function

  • The DSC OFF switch, located on the dashboard, allows for optionally enabling/disabling the TCS/DSC control at driver discretion.

  • The DSC OFF indicator light, built into the instrument cluster, informs the driver that TCS/DSC control has been disabled by operation of the DSC OFF switch.

Operation

  • When the DSC system and CAN lines are functionally normally, the DSC OFF indicator light illuminates for approx. 3.0 s when the ignition is switched ON to check the light function.

  • When the DSC OFF switch is pressed to disable TCS/DSC control, the DSC OFF indicator light illuminates.

  • The DSC OFF indicator light illumination and TCS/DSC control conditions while operating the DSC OFF switch are as indicated in the table.
