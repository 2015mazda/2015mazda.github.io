2014 - Mazda6 - Brakes

DLC-2 [DYNAMIC STABILITY CONTROL (DSC)]

OUTLINE

  • A connector (DLC-2) conforming to International Organization for Standardization (ISO) standards has been added.

CONSTRUCTION

  • Shape and terminal arrangement as stipulated by the ISO 15031-3 (SAE J1962) international standard has been adopted for this connector. The 16-pin connector consists of a CAN_H (HS) terminal, CAN_L (HS) terminal, CAN_H (MS) terminal, CAN_L (MS) terminal, GND1 terminal, GND2 terminal, and B+ terminal.
