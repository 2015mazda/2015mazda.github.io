2014 - Mazda6 - Steering

CONTROLLER AREA NETWORK (CAN) OUTLINE

  • The Electric Power Steering (EPS) control module sends and receives data to and from other modules via the CAN. (See CONTROLLER AREA NETWORK (CAN) SYSTEM.)

Data sent

  • EPS status

  • Power steering malfunction indicator light on request

  • Steering angle

Data received

  • Vehicle speed

  • Engine speed

  • Wheel-speed (LF, RF, LR, RR)

  • Steering angle
