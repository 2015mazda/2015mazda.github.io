2014 - Mazda6 - Steering

STEERING FEATURES



Improved handling, fuel economy, marketability

  • Column assist-type Electric Power Steering (EPS) adopted

Improved handling

  • By using a higher steering gear ratio (15.5), the steerability has been improved

Improved operability

  • Steering column with tilt/telescope mechanism adopted

Improved safety

  • Steering column and shaft with energy absorbing mechanism adopted

Improved serviceability

  • Enhanced malfunction diagnosis system for use with Mazda modular diagnostic system (M-MDS)
