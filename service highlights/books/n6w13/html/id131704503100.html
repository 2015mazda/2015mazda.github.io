2014 - Mazda6 - I-ELOOP

DC-DC CONVERTER [i-ELOOP]

Purpose, Function

  • The DC-DC converter supplies the electricity supplied from the capacitor (i-ELOOP) to the vehicle’s electrical devices.

  • If the electricity supplied from the capacitor (i-ELOOP) exceeds the rated voltage of an electrical device, the DC-DC converter lowers the voltage to 12 V and then supplies the electricity to the electrical devices.

Construction

  • The DC-DC converter is installed directly under the front seat (LH).

  • The DC-DC converter has the following inputs/outputs.



Terminal signal

Input/output

3A

Power is input from capacitor (i-ELOOP).

Power is output to capacitor (i-ELOOP). (Pre-charge)

4A

Power is output to battery and electrical device.

Power is input from battery. (Pre-charge)

1A

CAN communication

1B

GND

1C

CAN communication

1D

GND

1E

Temperature sensor signal input from capacitor (i-ELOOP).

1G

The by-pass relay on signal from the PCM is input.

1H

Battery power via IG1 relay is input.

1I

The by-pass relay on signal from the PCM is input.

  • The DC-DC converter switches the operation content according to the vehicle conditions. Refer to “i-ELOOP” for details. (See i-ELOOP [i-ELOOP].)

Fail-safe

    • Transition to i-ELOOP bypass mode.

    • i-ELOOP bypass mode inhibited.

    • i-ELOOP bypass mode inhibited.
