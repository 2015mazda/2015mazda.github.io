2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

REAR MOUNT CAMERA

Purpose, Function

  • The rear mount camera shoots the conditions at the rear of the vehicle in color, and after converting the video signal, it outputs the signal to the audio unit.

Construction

  • The rear mount camera is installed to the trunk lid.

  • A super-wide angle lens which can shoot images in a wide parameter is assembled to the compact (1/3.6 in), color CMOS camera which has a high precision CMOS *1 imaging element.

*1
Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor

Operation

  • For the rear mount camera operation, refer to the PARK ASSIST SYSTEM.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped
