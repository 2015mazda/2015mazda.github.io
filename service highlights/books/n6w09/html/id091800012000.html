HIGH-MOUNT BRAKE LIGHT

Purpose

  • The high-mount brake light signals vehicles to the rear that the vehicle is stopped.

Function

  • The high-mount brake light illuminates in conjunction with the operation of the brake switch.

Construction

  • The high-mount brake light is installed to the rear package trim.

  • The high-mount brake light has adopted a LED.

NOTE:

  • Fogging or condensation on the inside of the high-mount brake light may occur, however, it is a natural phenomenon occurring as a result of a temperature difference between the interior and exterior of the high-mount brake light and has no effect on the light performance. Fogging or condensation will dissipate when the temperature inside the high-mount brake light rises after the high-mount brake light is illuminated and a period of time has elapsed.

Operation

Operation due to brake pedal operation
    Without Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC) system

  • When the brake pedal is depressed, the brake switch (No.1 signal) turns on.

  • When the brake switch (No.1 signal) turns on, current flows from the battery to the high-mount brake light.

  • The high-mount brake light turns on.

    With Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC) system

  • When the brake pedal is depressed, the brake switch (No.1 signal) turns on.

  • When the brake switch (No.1 signal) turns on, a brake switch (No.1 signal) on signal is input to the brake light unit.

  • When the brake light unit receives a brake switch (No.1 signal) on signal, it supplies current to the high-mount brake light.

  • The high-mount brake light turn on.

Operation due to operation request signal from rear body control module (RBCM)

NOTE:

  • Refer to "SMART CITY BRAKE SUPPORT (SCBS)" and "MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) SYSTEM [MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) SYSTEM]" for details.

    Without Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC) system

  • When the operation conditions are met, the rear body control module (RBCM) turns on the transistor inside the rear body control module (RBCM).

  • When the transistor inside the rear body control module (RBCM) turns on, the brake light relay turns on.

  • When the brake light relay turns on, current flows from the battery to the high-mount brake light.

  • The high-mount brake light turns on.

    With Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC) system

  • When the operation conditions are met, the rear body control module (RBCM) turns on the transistor inside the rear body control module (RBCM).

  • When the transistor inside the rear body control module (RBCM) turns on, the transistor inside the brake light unit turns on.

  • When the transistor inside the brake light unit turns on, current flows to the high-mount brake light.

  • The high-mount brake light turn on.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
