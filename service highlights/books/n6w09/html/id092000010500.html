STEERING SWITCH

Purpose, Function

  • Operation of the audio system and change of the instrument cluster display are possible without a change in driving posture.

Construction

  • The steering switch is located on the spoke of the steering wheel.

  • The resistance is built into each switch.

Without Bluetooth system

  • The steering switch consists of a volume adjustment (+) or (-) switch, cue/automatic band selector (up) or (down) switch, INFO button, up switch, down switch, and mute button.

With Bluetooth system

  • The steering switch consists of a volume adjustment (+) or (-) switch, cue/automatic band selector (up) or (down) switch, INFO button, up switch, down switch, TALK button, PICK-UP button, and HANG-UP button.

Operation



Switch name

Operation

Volume adjustment switch

Audio volume can be adjusted.

Cue/Automatic band selector switch

Cue of CD tracks and selection of radio band are possible.

Mute button (Without Bluetooth system)

Audio volume can be muted. Pressing again after muting returns to previous volume level.

INFO button

Instrument cluster display can be changed. Refer to TRIP COMPUTER INFORMATION SYSTEM for display content.

Up/down switch

Instrument cluster display can be changed. Refer to TRIP COMPUTER INFORMATION SYSTEM for display content.

TALK button (With Bluetooth system)

Launches the Bluetooth system.

Pick-up button (With Bluetooth system)

Receives a phone call.

Hang-up button (With Bluetooth system)

Hangs up a phone call.


Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
