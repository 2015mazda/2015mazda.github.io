2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

HORN

Purpose

  • The horn sounds according to the horn switch operation.

Function

  • Sound is harmonized between high and low pitch.

Construction

  • Installed to the inside of the front grille.

  • The diaphragm is vibrated to apply vibration to the air and produce sound.

Operation

1. When the horn switch turns on (1), the horn relay turns on (2).
2. When the horn relay turns on, the horn sounds.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
