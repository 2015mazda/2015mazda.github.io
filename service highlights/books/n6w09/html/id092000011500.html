CAR-NAVIGATION UNIT

Purpose

  • Reads the SD card data inserted into the car-navigation unit and sends the read data to the audio unit.

Function

  • The car-navigation unit has the following functions:

TomTom Home

  • By connecting the SD card to your computer at home, the map data or software can be updated by the user.

Construction

  • The car-navigation unit is installed to the driver-side lower panel.

  • The panel is integrated with the unit.
