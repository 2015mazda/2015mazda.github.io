POWER SYSTEMS

Outline

  • System in which electric current from the battery supplies power to each electrical part.

  • There are mainly three types of electric power supply as follows, which are divided into system-supplied power and power used by parts.

    • Supply of direct power from battery

    • Supply of power by switching ignition ON (engine off)

    • Supply of power by switching ignition ON (engine on)
