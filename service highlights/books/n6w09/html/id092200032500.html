2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SPEEDOMETER

Purpose

  • The speedometer notifies the driver of the speed at which the vehicle is traveling.

Function

  • The instrument cluster controls the speedometer needle based on the vehicle speed signal from the PCM and displays the current vehicle speed.

Construction

  • An analog needle display speedometer with a stepping motor mechanism has been adopted.

Operation

  • The instrument cluster receives (1) the vehicle speed signal from the PCM when the ignition is switched ON (engine on).

  • The instrument cluster moves the needle based on the vehicle speed signal and displays (2) the current vehicle speed.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
