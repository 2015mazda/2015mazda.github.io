SIRIUS SATELLITE RADIO ANTENNA

Purpose, Function

  • Converts SIRIUS satellite radio broadcast waves to electric signals and sends the signals to the audio unit.

Construction

  • A SIRIUS satellite radio antenna has been adopted which does not require retraction or removal before going through an automatic car wash machine.

  • The SIRIUS satellite radio antenna has an integrated antenna amplifier which amplifies the electric signal.

Operation

  • When the ignition is switched to ACC or ON (engine off or on), power is supplied from the audio unit to the amplifier inside the SIRIUS satellite radio antenna.

  • The SIRIUS satellite radio antenna converts the received radio waves of a radio broadcast to an electric signal.

  • The SIRIUS satellite radio antenna amplifies the converted electric signal using the antenna amplifier and sends the signal to the audio unit.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
