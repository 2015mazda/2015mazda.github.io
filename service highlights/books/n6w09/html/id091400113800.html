TRUNK LID RELEASE ACTUATOR

Purpose, Function

  • The latch of the trunk lid latch lever is released based on the signal from the rear body control module (RBCM).

Construction

  • Motor is built-in.

  • Installed to the trunk lid latch and release actuator.

Operation

  • The trunk lid release actuator rotates the built-in motor based on the trunk lid opener system operation signal from the rear body control module (RBCM), and releases the latch of the trunk lid latch lever.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
