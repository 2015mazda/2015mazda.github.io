2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

HOOD

Purpose/Function

  • The hood structure is designed to absorb impact if the vehicle comes into contact with a pedestrian by assuring a space and using parts which deform.

Construction

  • To absorb impact if the vehicle comes into contact with a pedestrian, the hood has a hollow structure in the center area.

  • A deformable space is assured between the hood and engine.

  • If the hood receives an impact from above, the hood hinges installed to the hood collapse to mediate impact if a pedestrian contacts the hood.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
