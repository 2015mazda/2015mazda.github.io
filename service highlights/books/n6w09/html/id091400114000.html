TRUNK LID LATCH SWITCH

Purpose, Function

  • The switch turns on/off in conjunction with the rotation of the latch lever, and it detects the open/closed condition of the trunk lid by the voltage value which is changed by the rear body control module (RBCM).

Construction

  • The switch is turned on/off by the rotation of the latch lever.

  • When the trunk lid is completely closed, the switch turns off (contact point: Open)

Operation

Trunk lid closed (latched) operation
1. When the trunk lid is closed, the trunk lid lock striker enters the latch lever groove.
2. When the trunk lid further closes, the latch lever is pressed by the trunk lid lock striker and it rotates.
3. When the trunk lid is completely closed, the latch lever is in the latched condition pressing the trunk lid latch switch, which turns the switch off.
Trunk lid open (unlatch) operation

  • When the trunk lid release actuator operates to open the trunk lid, the latch lever is in an unlatched condition, and the trunk lid latch switch turns on.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
