2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

BODY PANEL

Outline

  • A straight structure has been adopted which is both highly rigid and light.

  • A multi-load path structure has been adopted which assures a high level of impact safety.

  • A ring structure has been adopted which improves the overall rigidity.

Structure View

Construction

Straight structure

  • The straight shape of the frame creates a continuous configuration from the front to the rear.

  • For sections of the frame that still require some curvature, continuous bonding with the horizontal frame was implemented to make the structure a closed section to the maximum extent possible.

Multi-load path structure

  • The impact force if the vehicle is involved in a collision is absorbed very efficiently by distributing the impact in multiple directions.

Ring structure

  • Four ring structures have been created for the upper body including the roof rail and B-pillar, and the entire reinforcement area of the underbody.

  • By creating a ring structure, cabin deformation control has been improved.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
