2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SUNROOF UNIT

Purpose / Function

  • The glass panel tilts up/down or slides open/closed by the operation of the sunroof unit.

Construction/Operation

  • The drive cables inside the frame of the sunroof unit are engaged to the sunroof motor drive gear.

  • When the sunroof motor rotates normally or in reverse, the drive cables operate in the same direction.

  • The glass panel is fixed to the guides so that the panel moves by the drive cables sliding the guides.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
