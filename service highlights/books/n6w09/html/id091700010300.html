2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

REAR CONSOLE

Outline

  • A sliding-type console lid is equipped with the rear console for enhanced passenger comfort.

Structural View

Construction

  • If the console lid is slid forward (2) while pulling up the release handle (1), the console lid can be used as an armrest.

  • CAUTION:

    • If the console lid is slid without pulling up the release handle, the lock part may be damaged and the console lid may not be secured. Always slide the console lid while pulling up the release handle.

    • If the console lid is slid with a cup left in the cup holder, the console lid may contact the cup and the liquid in the cup may spill. When a cup or a container containing liquid is in the cup holder, move the cup or the container aside before sliding the lid, or do not slide the console lid.
