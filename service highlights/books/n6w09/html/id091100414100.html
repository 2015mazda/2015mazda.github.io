2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SIDE IMPACT BAR

Purpose, Function

  • The side-impact bars prevent the doors from deforming inwards during a side-impact collision.

Construction

  • The front door side-impact bar lower and rear door side-impact bar lower are corrugated in shape to assure high rigidity.

  • The side-impact bars disperse the impact during a side-impact collision.

  • Inward penetration of the doors during a side-impact collision is prevented by directly connecting the upper and lower side-impact bars of the front doors directly to the front door hinge using bolts and positioning them such that they overlap the center pillar.

  • Inward penetration of the doors during a side-impact collision is prevented by directly connecting the lower side-impact bars of the rear doors directly to the rear door hinge using bolts and positioning them such that they overlap the side sill.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
