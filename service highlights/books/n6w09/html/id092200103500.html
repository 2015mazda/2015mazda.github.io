LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY (LCD)

Outline

  • The LCD displays vehicle information in the instrument cluster.

Function/Construction

  • The instrument cluster configures the indication of the LCD based on the CAN signals sent from the related modules and displays it.

  • The content displayed in the LCD differs depending on whether it is Type A or B.
