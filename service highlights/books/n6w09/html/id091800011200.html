HEADLIGHT LEVELING ACTUATOR

Purpose, Function

  • The headlight leveling actuator drives the motor based on the target optical axis voltage, which is input from the AFS control module, to change the optical axis of the headlights.

Construction

  • Built into the front combination lights.

  • Consists of a motor which expands/retracts the shaft, a shaft, and a microcomputer which calculates the shaft expansion/contraction amount.

Operation

  • The headlight leveling actuator calculates the motor drive amount based on the target optical axis signal from the AFS control module and drives the motor until the headlight reaches the target optical axis.

  • When the motor is driven, the shaft moves back and forth and the reflector moves to change the optical axis.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
