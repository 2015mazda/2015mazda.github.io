2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

ON/OFF SOLENOID [FW6A-EL]

Purpose/Function

  • The on/off solenoid performs switching of the shift valve in the control valve body according to the vehicle conditions.

Construction

  • The on/off solenoid is installed to the solenoid control valve body.

  • The on/off solenoid is a normal open-type on/off solenoid.

Operation

Non-energization (OFF)

  • When the on/off solenoid is off, the supply port in the solenoid opens. As a result, hydraulic pressure is supplied to operate the shift valve.

Energized (ON)

  • When the on/off solenoid is on, the drain port in the solenoid opens. As a result, hydraulic pressure which operates the shift valve is drained.
