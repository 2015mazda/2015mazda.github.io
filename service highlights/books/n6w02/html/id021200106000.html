2014 - Mazda6 - Suspension

WHEELS AND TIRES

Outline

  • Adhesive-type balance weights have been adopted to the outer side of the aluminum alloy wheels. Due to this, a stylish wheel design is realized.

  • In consideration of the environment, steel balance weights have been adopted to reduce the use of lead in the vehicle.

  • Tires with optimized characteristics and low rolling resistance have been adopted.
