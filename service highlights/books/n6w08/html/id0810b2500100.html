2014 - Mazda6 - Restraints

AIR BAG SYSTEM [STANDARD DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM]

Outline

  • The air bag system is a device that supplements the passenger restraint function of the seat belts. The air bag system will not have the designed effect if the seat belts are not worn properly.

  • The air bag system is composed of the following parts:



Item

Outline

SAS control module

  1. A yaw rate sensor and a low-G sensor has been added.

  2. Recognizes actually equipped air bag module or pre-tensioner seat belt based on module configuration.

Crash zone sensor

  • Detects degree of impact, converts it to an electrical signal, and sends the signal to the SAS control module.

Side air bag sensor

Driver-side air bag module

  • Adopted to improve safety in frontal collisions.

Passenger-side air bag module

Side air bag module

  • Chest-protection type side air bag module is used in accordance with the adoption of the curtain air bag module.

Curtain air bag module

  • Adopted to improve safety in lateral collisions.

Pre-tensioner seat belt

  • Gear-type pre-tensioner seat belt has been adopted.

Air bag system warning light

  • LED has been adopted.
