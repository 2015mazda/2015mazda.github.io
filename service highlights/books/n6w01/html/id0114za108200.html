2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

FUEL INJECTOR [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • Promotes atomization of fuel by injecting it from the multiple holes.

  • Injects fuel according to the control signal from the PCM.

Construction

  • The fuel injector is installed to the cylinder head.

  • The fuel injector has multiple injection holes in area A shown in the figure.

Operation

  • Injects fuel according to the signal from the PCM. The fuel injection time is determined by the energization time of the coil.

  • For fuel injection control details, refer to CONTROL SYSTEM. (See FUEL INJECTION CONTROL SYSTEM [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
