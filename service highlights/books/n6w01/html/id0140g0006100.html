2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

FUEL PRESSURE SENSOR [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose/Function

  • Detects the fuel pressure in the fuel distributor as basic information for mainly determining the fuel injection amount.

  • Detects the fuel pressure in the fuel distributor and sends it to the PCM as a fuel pressure signal.

Construction

  • Installed on the fuel distributor.

  • A metal diaphragm thin film distortion gauge has been adopted as the detecting element for detecting the fuel pressure.

Operation

  • Sends the signal, which fluctuates according to the fuel pressure, to the PCM.

Fuel pressure sensor characteristic

Fail-safe

Fail-safe function

  • Limits intake air amount
