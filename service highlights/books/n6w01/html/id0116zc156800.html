EVAPORATIVE CHAMBER [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • The evaporative chamber prevents penetration of water and dust in the charcoal canister.

Construction

  • The evaporative chamber is installed to the side of the charcoal canister.

    U.S.A. and CANADA

    Except U.S.A. and CANADA

  • A small section with partitions is located in the evaporative chamber.

Operation

  • There partitions protect the charcoal canister by preventing flooding as atmospheric air enters from the airflow holes.
