2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

MAIN RELAY [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose/Function

  • Supplies power to each part.

  • Supplies battery voltage to each part based on the signals from the PCM even though the ignition is switched on or off.

Construction

  • Installed in the relay and fuse block.

Operation

  • After the contact point is on battery voltage is supplied to the coil based on the signals from the PCM.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
