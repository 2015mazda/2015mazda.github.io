2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

FUEL TANK [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • Store fuel safely and supply fuel to the engine.

Construction

  • The fuel tank is installed below the rear seat.

  • The fuel tank is made of steel.

  • The following parts are integrated in the fuel tank.

      • U.S.A. and CANADA

    • Non-return valve

      (See NON-RETURN VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

    • Rollover valve

      (See ROLLOVER VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

    • Fuel shut-off valve

      (See FUEL SHUT-OFF VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

      Except U.S.A. and CANADA

    • Non-return valve

      (See NON-RETURN VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

    • Rollover valve

      (See ROLLOVER VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
