2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

CLUTCH PEDAL POSITION (CPP) SWITCH [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose/Function

  • Detects the connection status of the clutch.

Construction

  • Installed on the clutch pedal.

  • An ON/OFF type switch has been adopted.

Operation

  • Sends the CPP switch on/off signal to the PCM.

  • The contact point is on and voltage is output when the clutch pedal is engaged, and off when the clutch pedal is not engaged.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
