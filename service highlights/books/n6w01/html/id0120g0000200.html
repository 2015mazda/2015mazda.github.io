MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) DISPLAY [MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) SYSTEM]

Outline

  • The MRCC system uses the MRCC warning light/indicator light and LCD display to notify the driver of the MRCC status.

Construction

  • The MRCC warning light/indicator light and LCD are located in the instrument cluster.
