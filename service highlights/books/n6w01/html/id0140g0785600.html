2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (HO2S) HEATER CONTROL [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Outline

  • Based on the control of the HO2S heater, a stabilized oxygen concentration is detected even at low exhaust temperatures, and feedback control of fuel injection even at cold engine start is made possible for improved cold temperature exhaust emission performance.

  • Both emission performance and sensor protection have been improved by duty control of the heater according to engine operation conditions (exhaust gas temperature)

Block Diagram

Operation

  • The PCM operates the HO2S heater based on the following control conditions.



Output duty ratio

Control condition

0%

  • Engine stopped

  • Fuel cut injection control is in engine start zone

  • Engine coolant temperature is -10 °C {14 °F}or less

  • Other than battery voltage of 10—16 V

  • Within a fixed period of time after engine start (varies according to engine coolant temperature)

30―50%

  • HO2S not activated

0―100%

(Determined by engine speed and charging efficiency)

  • Other than the above
