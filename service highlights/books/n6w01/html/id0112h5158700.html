COOLING FAN RELAY [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • The cooling fan relay operates by receiving the signal from the PCM to supply power to the cooling fan motor.

Construction

  • The cooling fan relay is installed to the relay and fuse block.

Operation

  • The contact point is on when the battery voltage is supplied to the coil based on the signal from the PCM, and the battery voltage is supplied to the cooling fan motor.

Fail-safe

  • Function not equipped.
