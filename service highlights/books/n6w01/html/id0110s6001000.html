2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

CRANKSHAFT PULLEY [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • The crankshaft pulley transmits the drive force to the auxiliary parts via the drive belt.

  • The crankshaft pulley suppresses torsional vibration of the crankshaft.

Construction

  • The crankshaft pulley is installed to the front of the crankshaft.

  • The crankshaft pulley has a torsional damper for suppressing torsional vibration of the crankshaft.

  • The crankshaft pulley has key groove to match the timing to the crankshaft.

  • The crankshaft pulley has a timing mark indicating the piston position.
