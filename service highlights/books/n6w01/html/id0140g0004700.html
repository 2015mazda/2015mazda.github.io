2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (HO2S) [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose/Function

  • Detects the oxygen concentration (theoretical air/fuel ratio) in the exhaust gas as basic information for mainly determining the fuel injection amount and as information for determining the deterioration of catalytic converter.

  • Detects the oxygen concentration (theoretical air/fuel ratio) in the exhaust gas and sends it to the PCM as an oxygen concentration (theoretical air/fuel ratio) signal.

Construction

  • Installed on the rear side of the exhaust manifold.

  • A zirconia element has been adopted as the detecting element for detecting the oxygen concentration (theoretical air/fuel ratio).

Operation

  • Sends the signal, which fluctuates according to the oxygen concentration (theoretical air/fuel ratio), to the PCM.

  • The PCM applies approx. 1.5 V to the HO2S input voltage to prevent incorrect diagnosis of the HO2S input voltage.
