2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

RADIATOR [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • The radiator disperses the engine coolant heat flowing internally by receiving the air flow against the vehicle while it is driven or the air flow produced by the cooling fan.

Construction

  • The radiator is installed to the front of the engine compartment.

  • Four mounting rubbers are utilized to decrease vibration.

  • A down-flow radiator with corrugated fins has been adopted.

  • The radiator tanks are made of plastic and the core is made of aluminum for weight reduction.
