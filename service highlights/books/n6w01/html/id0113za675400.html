2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

FRESH-AIR DUCT [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • Conducts fresh air from outside the engine compartment.

  • Increases air density by taking fresh air, which is cooler than the engine compartment, to enhance the charging efficiency, ensuring the combustion stability.

Construction

  • The fresh-air duct is installed to the air cleaner.
