2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

POSITIVE CRANKCASE VENTILATION (PCV) VALVE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • Prevents release of blow-by gas (unburnt gas) into the atmosphere.

  • The PCV valve is open/closed by the intake manifold vacuum.

Construction

  • The PCV valve is installed to the oil separator.

  • The PCV valve consists of a spring and valve.

Operation

  • When the valve is pulled by the intake manifold vacuum, blow-by gas flows to the intake manifold.

  • Blow-by gas flow amount is adjusted by the spring according to the intake manifold vacuum.
