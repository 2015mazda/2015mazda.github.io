FUEL PUMP CONTROL MODULE [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

Purpose, Function

  • Controls the voltage applied to the fuel pump for reducing fuel pump load and improving fuel economy.

  • Controls the voltage applied to the fuel pump based on the control signal from the PCM.

Construction

  • The fuel pump control module is installed below the rear seat.

Operation

  • Controls the voltage applied to the fuel pump based on the control signal from the PCM. In addition, the on-board diagnostic function is equipped and if there is a malfunction in the fuel pump control module, a diagnostic signal is sent to the PCM. The PCM stores a DTC if necessary according to the received diagnostic signal.

  • For the fuel pump control module, refer to CONTROL SYSTEM. (See FUEL PUMP CONTROL [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

Fail-safe



P025B:00

  • Stops the fuel pump control (when overheating is detected).

P0629:00

  • Stops the fuel pump control.
