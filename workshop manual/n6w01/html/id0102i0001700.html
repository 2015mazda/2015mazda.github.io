2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

DTC C1A67:78 [VEHICLE CONTROL MODULE (V/C-MODULE)]



System malfunction location

Radar sensor axis is deviated

Detection condition

  • Radar sensor axis deviation is detected.

Fail-safe

  • Inhibits the Mazda radar cruise control (MRCC) system control

  • Inhibits the forward obstruction warning (FOW) system control

Possible cause

  • Radiator grille on vehicle with radar sensor not installed

  • Dirt on radiator grille ornament, modifications, application of stickers (including transparent types), scratches, or damage

  • Dirt on radar sensor, scratches, or damage

  • Impact is transmitted to area around radar sensor

  • Incorrect installation of radar sensor

  • Radar sensor malfunction

  • DSC HU/CM malfunction

  • SAS control module malfunction

  • Vehicle control module (V/C-module) malfunction

System wiring diagram

  • Not applicable

Diagnostic Procedure



Step

Inspection

Action

1

 VERIFY RADIATOR GRILLE

  • Is the radiator on a vehicle with a radar sensor installed?

Yes

Go to the next step.

No

Replace the radiator grille on the vehicle with a radar sensor, then go to Step 9.

2

 VERIFY AREA AROUND RADIATOR GRILLE ORNAMENT

  • Visually inspect the surface of the radiator grille ornament.

  • Under the following conditions?

    • Dirt is adhering to radiator grille ornament

    • Foreign matter (reflective object) is adhering to radiator grille ornament

    • Radiator grille ornament is scratched or damaged

Yes

Return the surfaces of the radiator grille ornament to their normal condition, then go to Step 9.

No

Go to the next step.

3

 VERIFY AREA AROUND RADAR SENSOR

  • Remove front bumper.

    (See FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  • Visually inspect the surface of the radar sensor.

  • Under the following conditions?

    • Dirt is adhering to radar sensor

    • Foreign matter (reflective object) is adhering to radar sensor

    • Radar sensor is scratched or damaged

Yes

Return the radar sensor to their normal condition, then go to Step 9.

No

Go to the next step.

4

 VERIFY LCD

  • Is the message indicating “FOW, MRCC Radar blocked” displayed in the LCD in the instrument cluster?

Yes

Switch the ignition off (LOCK), leave it for 30 min to cool down the radar sensor, switch the ignition ON (engine off or on) again, and verify the message indicating “FOW, MRCC, Radar blocked” in the LCD.

    “FOW, MRCC Radar blocked” is not displayed

  • Go to the next step.

    “FOW, MRCC Radar blocked” is displayed

No

Go to the next step.

Yes

Go to the next step.

No

(See RADAR SENSOR AIMING [MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) SYSTEM].)

Then go to Step 9.

  • Clear vehicle control module (V/C-module) DTCs using the M-MDS.

  • Switch the ignition ON (engine on).

    (See DTC INSPECTION [VEHICLE CONTROL MODULE (V/C-MODULE)].)

  • Is the same DTC present?

Yes

Repeat the inspection from Step 1.

  • If the malfunction does not recur, go to the next step.

  • If the malfunction recurs, replace the vehicle control module (V/C-module).

    (See VEHICLE CONTROL MODULE (V/C-MODULE) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [MAZDA RADAR CRUISE CONTROL (MRCC) SYSTEM].)

Go to the next step.

10

 VERIFY THAT NO OTHER DTCs ARE PRESENT

Yes

No

DTC troubleshooting completed.
