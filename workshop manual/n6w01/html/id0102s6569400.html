2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

DTC P1794:00 [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

NOTE:

  • To determine the malfunctioning part, proceed with the diagnostics from “Function Inspection Using M-MDS”.

Details On DTCs



DESCRIPTION

Capacitor (i-ELOOP) system: Capacitor (i-ELOOP) malfunction

DETECTION CONDITION

Determination conditions

  • Capacitor malfunction determined

    • Any one of the following conditions is met:

      • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) internal resistance exceeds the specification.

      • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) capacitance exceeds the specification.

      • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) voltage exceeds the specification.

  • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) deterioration determined

    • Any one of the following conditions is met:

      • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) internal resistance exceeds the specification.

      • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) capacitance is less the than specification.

  • Over-charge determined

    • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) voltage exceeds the specification.

Preconditions

  • Not applicable.

Drive cycle

  • 1

Self test type

  • CMDTC self test

Sensor used

  • DC-DC converter

FAIL-SAFE FUNCTION

  • Inhibits the i-ELOOP control.

VEHICLE STATUS WHEN DTCs ARE OUTPUT

  • The following vehicle conditions differ depending on the type of malfunction

    • Master warning light is illuminated (illumination during bypass mode by i-ELOOP)

    • Charging system warning light is illuminated (illumination while generator output is inhibited)

    • Pre-charge warning is displayed after the engine is started.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

  • Engagement malfunction in capacitor (i-ELOOP), DC-DC converter

  • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) malfunction

    • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) deterioration

  • Generator overcharge (in this case, PCM detects DTC P2504:00)

  • DC-DC converter malfunction

  • PCM malfunction

System Wiring Diagram

  • Not applicable.

Function Explanation (DTC Detection Outline)

  • The capacitor (i-ELOOP) can store generated electrical energy from the generator efficiently.

  • The electrical energy stored during deceleration fuel-cut is used during driving, which reduces the power generating load on the generator and the amount of fuel consumption.

  • If a malfunction occurs with the capacitor (i-ELOOP), constant charging takes place by operation of the bypass mode which could cause the fuel economy to worsen.

  • In this diagnostic, the internal resistance/capacitance is calculated from the capacitor (i-ELOOP) voltage value and charge/discharge current value, and the PCM determines that there is a malfunction in the battery-related parts by the internal resistance/capacitance/capacitor (i-ELOOP) voltage exceeding the specified value, and it stores a DTC.

Repeatability Verification Procedure

  • Start the engine.(no electrical load)

  • Wait for 20 min (idle).

  • Depress the accelerator pedal to increase the engine speed to 3,000 rpm.

  • Release the accelerator pedal to idle the engine.

  • Stop the engine.

  • Switch the ignition on and wait for 1 min.

  • Switch the ignition off and wait for 20 min.

  • Switch the ignition on.
