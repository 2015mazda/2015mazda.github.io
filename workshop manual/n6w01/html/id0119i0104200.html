2014 - Mazda6 - Engine

STARTER INTERLOCK SWITCH INSPECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

CAUTION:

  1. Do not reuse the starter interlock switch if it is removed from the vehicle even once. Replace with a new starter interlock switch when installing.

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Disconnect the starter interlock switch connector.
3. Verify that the continuity is as indicated in the table using a tester.

  • If the continuity is not as indicated in the table, replace the starter interlock switch.

    (See CLUTCH PEDAL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [C66M-R].)
