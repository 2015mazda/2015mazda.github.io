CANISTER VENT (CV) SOLENOID VALVE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5]

U.S.A. And CANADA

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the floor under cover (LH). (See FLOOR UNDER COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Disconnect the evaporative hose No.2. (See CHARCOAL CANISTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
4. Remove in the order indicated in the table.



1

CV solenoid valve connector

2

CV solenoid valve

3

O-ring

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
