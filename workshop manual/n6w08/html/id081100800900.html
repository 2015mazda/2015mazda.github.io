2014 - Mazda6 - Restraints

REAR CENTER SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

CAUTION:

  • If the retractor is disassembled, it will not operate correctly due to looseness and excessive play of the internal parts, therefore, do not disassemble the retractor.

1. Remove the following parts:
a. Rear seat cushion (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Rear side seat back (See REAR SIDE SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
d. Tire house trim (See TIRE HOUSE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
e. C-pillar trim (See C-PILLAR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
f. Rear package trim (See REAR PACKAGE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Remove the bolts.

3. Remove the rear center seat belt with the rear buckle (LH).
4. Install in the reverse order of removal. (See Rear Center Seat Belt Installation Note.)
