REAR SHOCK ABSORBER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Remove in the order indicated in the table.
2. Install in the reverse order of removal.



1

Rear shock absorber upper nut

(See Rear Shock Absorber Upper Nut Installation Note.)

2

Rear shock absorber lower bolt (Type A)

(See Rear Shock Absorber Lower Bolt Installation Note.)

3

Rear shock absorber lower nut (Type B)

4

Washer (Type B)

5

Stud bolt (Type B)

6

Rear shock absorber

Rear Shock Absorber Lower Bolt Installation Note

1. Insert the rear shock absorber bushing into the damper pin as shown in the following figure, engage the notches of the damper pin and the washer correctly, and install the rear shock absorber lower bolt.
