2014 - Mazda6 - Suspension

FRONT LOWER ARM INSPECTION

1. Remove the front lower arm. (See FRONT LOWER ARM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Inspect the front lower arm for bending or damage, and the front lower arm ball joint for excessive looseness.

  • If there is any malfunction, replace the front lower arm.

3. Rotate the front lower arm ball joint stud 5 times. Install the SST to the front lower arm ball joint stud, measure the rotational, starting torque using a torque wrench.

  • If not within the specification, replace the front lower arm.

    Front lower arm ball joint stud starting torque

  • 18 N·m {1.8 kgf·m,13 ft·lbf} max.

    Front lower arm ball joint stud rotational torque

  • 3.2 N·m {33 kgf·cm,28 in·lbf} max.
