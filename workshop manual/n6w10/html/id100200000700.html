2014 - Mazda6 - Multiplex Communication System

DETERMINING SHORT TO POWER SUPPLY LOCATION (HS-CAN)

CAUTION:

  • Perform the following malfunction diagnosis only when it is diagnosed with a short to power supply by CONTROLLER AREA NETWORK (CAN) MALFUNCTION DIAGNOSIS FLOW.

System wiring diagram

Determination procedure

CAUTION:

  • When disconnecting the connector, verify that there is no looseness, damage, deformation, corrosion, or poor connection of the connector terminals.

  • When inspecting the DLC-2, touch it with a paper clip or similar thin pin without directly inserting a tester into the terminals.

  • Disconnect the negative battery cable before performing any work that requires handling of connectors.

NOTE:

  • If the ignition does not turn on even if the ignition is switched ON, switch the ignition ON referring to the forced ignition ON procedure. (See FORCED IGNITION ON.)

40

 INSPECT DSC HU/CM FOR SHORT TO POWER SUPPLY

  • Switch the ignition off (LOCK).

  • Disconnect the negative battery cable.

    (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • Connect connector C-32.

  • Disconnect the DSC HU/CM connector.

  • Connect the negative battery cable.

    (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • Switch the ignition ON (engine off).

  • Measure the voltage at DLC-2 terminals F and E.

  • Is the voltage between 1.5 — 3.5 V?

Yes

Replace the DSC HU/CM because there is a short to the power supply in the DSC HU/CM.

(See DSC HU/CM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

No

Repair or replace the wiring harness between the DSC HU/CM and connector C-32 because the wiring harness is shorted to the power supply.

41

 INSPECT PCM FOR SHORT TO POWER SUPPLY

  • Switch the ignition off (LOCK).

  • Disconnect the negative battery cable.

    (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • Connect connector C-32.

  • Disconnect the PCM connector.

  • Connect the negative battery cable.

    (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • Switch the ignition ON (engine off).

  • Measure the voltage at DLC-2 terminals F and E.

  • Is the voltage between 1.5 — 3.5 V?

Yes

Replace the PCM because there is a short to the power supply in the PCM.

(See PCM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

No

Repair or replace the wiring harness between the PCM and connector C-32 because the wiring harness is shorted to the power supply.
