AIR MIX ACTUATOR INSPECTION [MANUAL AIR CONDITIONER]

CAUTION:

  • If the lever position exceeds the operation range shown in the figure, the circuit in the actuator could be damaged. Always perform an actuator operation inspection with the lever movement within the range shown in the figure.

1. Connect battery positive voltage to air mix actuator terminal C (or B), connect terminal B (or C) to ground, and then verify that the air mix actuator operates as shown in the table.

  • If the operation condition is not normal, replace the air mix actuator.
