DSC HU/CM INSPECTION

1. Disconnect the DSC HU/CM connector. (See DSC HU/CM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Connect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
3. Attach the tester lead to the DSC HU/CM wiring harness-side connector and inspect the voltage, continuity, or resistance according to the standard (reference) on the table.
