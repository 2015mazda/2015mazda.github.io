2014 - Mazda6 - Brakes

BRAKE SWITCH INSPECTION

CAUTION:

  • Inspect the brake switch with it installed to the brake pedal, otherwise the brake switch may not operate normally. If the brake switch is removed from the brake pedal, replace the brake switch with a new one.

1. Remove the lower panel. (See LOWER PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Remove the front heat duct (LH). (See FRONT HEAT DUCT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Disconnect the brake switch connector.
4. Verify that the continuity is as indicated in the table.

  • If not as indicated in the table, replace the brake switch. (See BRAKE PEDAL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
