2014 - Mazda6 - Brakes

VACUUM PUMP INSPECTION

1. Pinch open the clamp using pliers and disconnect the vacuum hose from the vacuum pump. (See VACUUM HOSE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Install the vacuum gauge as shown in the figure, then check the vacuum.

3. Warm up the engine.

  • If the pressure is less than the specification, inspect for the following.

    • Malfunction of the vacuum pump

    • Shortage of lubrication oil pressure
