2014 - Mazda6 - Brakes

PARKING BRAKE LEVER INSPECTION

Stroke Inspection

1. Pump the brake pedal a few times.
2. Pull the parking brake lever two to three times.
3. Inspect the parking brake stroke by slowly pulling at point A 50 mm {2.0 in} from the end of the parking brake lever with a force of 98 N {10 kgf, 22 lbf} and counting the number of notches (clicking sound).

  • If not within the specification, adjust the parking brake lever.

    Parking brake lever stroke when pulled at 98 N {10 kgf, 22 lbf}

  • 2—3 notches
