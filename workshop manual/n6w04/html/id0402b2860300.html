DTC

C0061:27, C0061:28, C0061:29, C0061:62, C0062:27, C0062:28, C0062:29, C0062:62, C0062:65, C0063:27, C0063:28, C0063:29, C0063:61, C0063:62, C006A:61

SAS control module system

DETECTION CONDITION

NOTE:

  • The low-G sensor and the yaw rate sensor are built into the SAS control module.

  • C0061:27, C0061:28, C0061:29

    • Out-of-specification signal modulation or lateral-G value is detected from the low-G sensor.

  • C0061:62

    • While driving at a vehicle speed of 10 km/h {6.2 mph} or more, the difference between the lateral-G value calculated from the yaw rate sensor value and the lateral-G value calculated from the low-G sensor value exceeds 0.5 G for a continuous 60 s.

  • C0062:27, C0062:28, C0062:29

    • Out-of-specification signal modulation or longitudinal-G value is detected from the low-G sensor.

  • C0062:62

    • While driving at a vehicle speed of 30 km/h {19 mph} or more, the difference between the longitudinal-G value calculated from the low-G sensor value and the longitudinal-G value calculated from the wheel speed exceeds 0.35 G for a continuous 60 s.

  • C0062:65

    • When the vehicle is stopped from a vehicle speed of 30 km/h {19 mph} or more, the longitudinal-G value of the low-G sensor remains unchanged for 16 times.

  • C0063:27, C0063:28, C0063:29

    • Out-of-specification signal modulation or yaw rate value is detected from the yaw rate sensor.

  • C0063:61

    • The difference between the estimated yaw rate values determined by the low-G sensor, and the EPS CM and the output value from the yaw rate sensor exceeds the specification.

  • C0063:62

    • While driving at a vehicle speed of 15 km/h {9.3 mph} or more, the difference between the yaw rate value calculated from the ABS wheel speed sensor and the low-G sensor and the yaw rate value from the yaw rate sensor exceeds the specification for a continuous 10 s.

  • C006A:61

    • The signals from the yaw rate sensor and the low-G sensor (lateral-G/longitudinal-G) do not change for 1 s or more.

FAIL-SAFE FUNCTION

  • C0061:27, C0061:28, C0061:29

    • Illuminates the ABS warning light, TCS/DSC indicator light, and master warning light*1.

    • Tire pressure monitoring system warning light illuminates after flashes.

    • Inhibits the ABS, brake assist, TCS, DSC, Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC)*2, Smart City Brake Support (SCBS)*2, and TPMS controls.

  • C0061:62, C0063:27, C0063:28, C0063:29, C0063:61, C0063:62

    • Illuminates the TCS/DSC indicator light, and master warning light*1.

    • Tire pressure monitoring system warning light illuminates after flashes.

    • Inhibits the TCS, DSC, Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC)*2, Smart City Brake Support (SCBS)*2, and TPMS controls.

  • C0062:27, C0062:28, C0062:29, C0062:62, C0062:65

    • Illuminates the ABS, TCS/DSC indicator light, and master warning light*1.

    • Inhibits the ABS, brake assist, TCS, hill launch assist (HLA), vehicle roll prevention function*2, DSC, Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC)*2, and Smart City Brake Support (SCBS)*2 controls.

  • C006A:61

    • Illuminates the ABS warning light, TCS/DSC indicator light, and master warning light*1.

    • Tire pressure monitoring system warning light illuminates after flashes*3.

    • Inhibits the ABS, brake assist, TCS, hill launch assist (HLA)*4, vehicle roll prevention function*2*4, DSC, Mazda Radar Cruise Control (MRCC)*2, Smart City Brake Support (SCBS)*2, and TPMS*4 controls.

*1: Vehicles with Smart City Brake Support (SCBS).

*2: If equipped.

*3: Not illuminated depending on malfunction content.

*4: Control enabled depending on malfunction content.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

  • SAS control module malfunction

  • Poor SAS control module installation

  • DSC HU/CM malfunction

SYSTEM WIRING DIAGRAM

Not applicable
