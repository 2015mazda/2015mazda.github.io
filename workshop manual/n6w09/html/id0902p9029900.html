2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

DTC U0001:88/U0100:00/U0121:00/U0131:00/U0140:00/U0155:00/U0214:00 [FORWARD SENSING CAMERA (FSC)]



Description

  • U0001:88

    • Module communication error (HS-CAN)

  • U0100:00

    • Communication error with PCM

  • U0121:00

    • Communication error with DSC HU/CM

  • U0131:00

    • Communication error with EPS control module

  • U0140:00

    • Communication error with front body control module (FBCM)

  • U0155:00

    • Communication error with instrument cluster

  • U0214:00

    • Communication error with start stop unit

Detection condition

  • U0001:88

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN bus communication signal (HS-CAN) for 1 s or more.

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) detects the CAN bus communication line (HS-CAN) malfunction 10 times continuously.

  • U0100:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the PCM for 1 s or more.

  • U0121:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the DSC HU/CM for 1 s or more.

  • U0131:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the EPS control module for 1 s or more.

  • U0140:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the front body control module (FBCM) for 5 s or more.

  • U0155:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the instrument cluster for 5 s or more.

  • U0214:00

    • Forward sensing camera (FSC) cannot receive the CAN signal from the start stop unit for 1 s or more.

Fail-safe function

  • U0001:88

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

  • U0100:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

  • U0121:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

  • U0131:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

  • U0140:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

  • U0155:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

  • U0214:00

    • Inhibits the lane departure warning system (LDWS).

    • Inhibits the high beam control (HBC) system.

Possible cause

  • Malfunction in CAN bus communication line

  • Malfunction in CAN line between PCM and forward sensing camera (FSC)

  • Malfunction in CAN line between DSC HU/CM and forward sensing camera (FSC)

  • Malfunction in CAN line between EPS control module and forward sensing camera (FSC)

  • Malfunction in CAN line between front body control module (FBCM) and forward sensing camera (FSC)

  • Malfunction in CAN line between instrument cluster and forward sensing camera (FSC)

  • Malfunction in CAN line between start stop unit and forward sensing camera (FSC)

System wiring diagram

Not applicable

Diagnostic Procedure

  • Perform the malfunction diagnosis according to the troubleshooting procedure for the multiplex communication system. (See FOREWORD.)
