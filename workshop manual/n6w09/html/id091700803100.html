TRUNK SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Remove the following parts:
a. Trunk covering (See TRUNK COVERING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Trunk board (See TRUNK BOARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Trunk side pocket (See TRUNK SIDE POCKET REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
d. Trunk end trim (See TRUNK END TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
e. Rear seat cushion (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
f. Rear side seat back (See REAR SIDE SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Remove fasteners A.

3. Remove the trunk side trim in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
