SUNROOF SYSTEM INITIALIZATION PROCEDURE

  • If the following operations have been performed, the initial setting is reset, and operations except for tilt up are disabled. Therefore, performing initial setting is necessary.

    • Negative battery cable is disconnected. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

    • Sunroof switch connector is disconnected.

    • Sunroof system power supply fuse is removed.

1. Switch the ignition ON (engine off or on).
2. Press the tilt up switch to fully tilt up the glass panel.
