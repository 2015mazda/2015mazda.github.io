SEAT WARMER CONTROL UNIT INSPECTION

WARNING:

  • Handling a side air bag improperly can accidentally operate (deploy) the air bag, which may seriously injure you. Read the service warnings/cautions in the Workshop Manual before handling the front seat (side air bag integrated). (See AIR BAG SYSTEM SERVICE WARNINGS [TWO-STEP DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].) (See AIR BAG SYSTEM SERVICE CAUTIONS [TWO-STEP DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].) (See AIR BAG SYSTEM SERVICE WARNINGS [STANDARD DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].) (See AIR BAG SYSTEM SERVICE CAUTIONS [STANDARD DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].)

  • If the sliding mechanisms on both sides are not locked after assembling the front seat, the front seat will operate accidentally while the vehicle is driven, which could result in serious injury. After assembling the front seat, shake it up and down and verify that the sliding mechanism on the both sides are locked.

CAUTION:

  • If the slide lever is operated after the front seat is removed, the left/right slide positions will deviate and the adjuster unit could be damaged after the front seat is installed. After removing a front seat, do not operate the slide lever.

  • Verify that there are no malfunctions in the forward/back slide of the seat and in the adjuster unit after installing a front seat.

  • When performing the procedure with a front seat removed from the vehicle, perform the procedure on a clean cloth so as not to damage or soil the seat.

  • When the passenger's seat is removed, perform the seat weight sensor inspection using the M-MDS after installing the passenger's seat. (See SEAT WEIGHT SENSOR INSPECTION [TWO-STEP DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].)

  • When the passenger's seat is replaced with a new one, perform the seat weight sensor calibration using the M-MDS. (See SEAT WEIGHT SENSOR CALIBRATION [TWO-STEP DEPLOYMENT CONTROL SYSTEM].)

Forward

  • When performing an asterisked (*) troubleshooting inspection, shake the wiring harness and connectors while performing the inspection to discover whether poor contact points are the cause of any intermittent malfunction. If there is a problem, inspect to make sure connectors, terminals and wiring harnesses are connected correctly and undamaged.

Inspection Procedure (No.1 Seat Warmer Function Is Inoperative)



No.1

Seat warmer function is inoperative

DETECTION

Seat warmer does not warm seat even though seat warmer switch is on

POSSIBLE CAUSE

    The power supply is not supplied to filament of seat warmer

  • Open circuit in seat warmer control unit power supply circuit:

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal O and battery positive terminal

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal M and battery positive terminal

  • SEAT WARM 20 A fuse malfunction (Blown fuse):

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal O and battery positive terminal

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal M and battery positive terminal

  • Poor connect the connector:

    • Driver-side seat warmer unit connector and seat warmer control unit connector

    • Passenger-side seat warmer unit connector and seat warmer control unit connector

  • Open circuit in wiring harness between the following terminal:

    • Driver-side seat warmer unit terminal P and driver-side seat warmer unit terminal A

    • Passenger-side seat warmer unit terminal N and passenger-side seat warmer unit terminal B

    Seat warmer control unit not operation

  • Open circuit or short to ground in wiring harness between the following terminal:

    • FBCM terminal 1C and seat warmer control unit terminal K

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal H and body ground

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal G and body ground

    Seat warmer unit cannot control filament on

  • Seat warmer unit malfunction

    Seat warmer filament does not generate heat

  • Driver-side seat warmer unit (seat cushion side) malfunction

  • Driver-side seat warmer unit (seat back side) malfunction

  • Passenger-side seat warmer unit (seat cushion side) malfunction

  • Passenger-side seat warmer unit (seat back side) malfunction

    Seat warmer control unit cannot receive operation signal

  • Seat warmer control unit malfunction

  • Open circuit or short to ground in wiring harness between the following terminal:

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal I and climate control unit terminal 1R (with full-auto air conditioner)

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal I and clock terminal D (with manual air conditioner)

Inspection Procedure (No.2 Seat Warmer Operates Constantly)



No.2

Seat warmer operates constantly

DETECTION

Seat warmer warms seat even though seat warmer switch is off

POSSIBLE CAUSE

    Seat warmer switch is always on

  • Seat warmer switch malfunction

    Seat warmer control unit falsely detects seat warmer switch as on

  • Seat warmer control unit malfunction

  • Short circuit in wiring harness between the following terminal:

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal I and climate control unit terminal 1R (with full-auto air conditioner)

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal I and clock terminal D (with manual air conditioner)

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

1*

 DETERMINE IF THE MALFUNCTION CAUSE IS THE SEAT WARMER SWITCH

  • Switch the ignition off (LOCK).

    With full-auto air conditioner

  • Disconnect the climate control unit connector.

    With manual air conditioner

  • Disconnect the clock connector.

  • Switch the ignition ON (engine off or on).

  • Is the seat warmer system operating?

Yes

Go to the next step.

No
    With full-auto air conditioner

  • Replace the climate control unit.

    (See CLIMATE CONTROL UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [FULL-AUTO AIR CONDITIONER].)

    With manual air conditioner

  • Replace the clock.

    (See CLOCK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2

 DETERMINE IF THE MALFUNCTION CAUSE IS THE SEAT WARMER COTROL UNIT

  • Disconnect the seat warmer control unit connector.

  • Measure the voltage between seat warmer control unit terminal I and body ground.

  • Is the voltage 1.0 or more?

Yes

Repair or replace for a short to ground in the wiring harness.

No

Replace the front seat.

(See FRONT SEAT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

Inspection Procedure (No.3 Temperature Of Seat Warmer Cannot Be Adjusted)



No.3

Temperature of seat warmer cannot be adjusted

DETECTION

  • Seat warmer does not adjust temperature even though seat warmer Lo/Mid/Hi switch is switched between low and high

  • Seat warmer temperature continues to rise

POSSIBLE CAUSE

    Seat warmer control unit cannot control seat warmer unit

  • Seat warmer control unit malfunction

  • Thermostat (Built-in seat warmer unit) malfunction

  • Short to ground circuit in wiring harness between the following terminal:

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal C and E (Driver-side)

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal D and F (Passenger-side)

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal C and body ground (Driver-side)

    • Seat warmer control unit terminal D and body ground (Passenger-side)
