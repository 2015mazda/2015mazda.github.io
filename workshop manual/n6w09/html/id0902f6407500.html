2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

DTC: B1022:07 [AUDIO]



Description

Audio unit internal malfunction

Detection condition

Communication error between the audio unit and audio panel

Possible cause

  • Audio panel malfunction

  • Audio unit malfunction

System wiring diagram

Not applicable

Diagnostic Procedure

Step

Inspection

Action

1
    INSPECT AUDIO PANEL

  • Launch the switch inspection mode.

    (See DIAGNOSTIC ASSIST FUNCTION [AUDIO].)

  • Does the buzzer sound when a switch is pressed?

Yes

Go to Step 3.

No

Replace the audio panel, then go to the next step.

(See AUDIO UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

2
    VERIFY TROUBLESHOOTING COMPLETED

  • Clear the DTC from the audio unit memory.

    (See CLEARING DTC [AUDIO].)

  • Perform the audio unit DTC inspection.

    (See DTC INSPECTION [AUDIO].)

  • Is the same DTC displayed?

Yes

Replace the audio panel, then go to the next step.

(See AUDIO UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

No

Go to the next step.

3
    VERIFY THAT NO OTHER DTCs ARE RECORDED

  • Are any other DTCs displayed?

Yes

Go to the applicable DTC inspection.

(See DTC TABLE [AUDIO].)

No

DTC troubleshooting completed.
