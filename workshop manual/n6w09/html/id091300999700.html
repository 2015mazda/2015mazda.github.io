REAR SEAT CUSHION LOCK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Remove the rear seat cushion. (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Move the flathead screwdriver wrapped in protective tape in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure and disengage the tab.

3. Remove the rear seat cushion lock in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
