2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SOUND JUMPS [CD PLAYER]



Possible DTC

Sound jumps

Using the M-MDS

B1188:64

Without using M-MDS (On-board diagnostic test mode)

10:Er07

Possible cause

  • Defective CD (e.g., cracked, badly bent, rough edges, scratch, dirty CD, condensation)

  • Improper audio unit installation (e.g., rattle, loose)

  • Inadequate tire pressure

  • Audio unit malfunction

NOTE:

  • The CD may be malfunctioning if the sound jumps on the certain CD only. Inspect the CD player/changer operation using the CD known to be good.

Diagnostic procedure

INSPECTION

ACTION

  • Does the sound skip when pressing the SCAN button?

Yes

No

Go to the next step.

2

  • Can the disc in which the malfunction occurs be specified?

Yes

The system is normal. (Explain to the customer that the cause is a disc that is damaged or dirty on the playback side, or warped.)

No

Go to the next step.

  • Does the sound jump when the vehicle is stopped?

Yes

Go to Step 8.

No

Go to the next step.

  • Drive the vehicle.

Go to the next step.

No

  • Adjust the tire pressure within specification.

  • Does the sound jump when driving the vehicle?

Yes

Replace the audio unit.

Yes
