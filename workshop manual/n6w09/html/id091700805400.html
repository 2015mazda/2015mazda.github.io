2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SIDE WALL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Pull the side wall in the direction of the arrow in the order of (1), (2) and remove it while detaching hooks A and clip B.

2. Pull the side wall in the direction of the arrow (3) and remove it while detaching guide C.

CAUTION:

  • If hooks A and clip B are removed forcefully, guide C may be damaged. Carefully remove hooks A and clip B so as not to damage guide C.

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
