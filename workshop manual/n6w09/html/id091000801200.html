HOOD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

WARNING:

  • Removing the hood without supporting it could cause the hood to fall and cause serious injury. Always perform the procedure with at least another person to prevent the hood from falling.

1. Loosen bolts A.

2. Remove bolts B.
3. Remove the hood in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure.
4. Remove bolts C.

5. Move the hood hinge in the direction of the arrow (2), (3) shown in the figure, and remove it.
6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
7. Perform the hood adjustment. (See HOOD ADJUSTMENT.)
