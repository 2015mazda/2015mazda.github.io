FRONT BODY CONTROL MODULE (FBCM) INSPECTION

1. Remove the fuse box cover. (See RELAY AND FUSE BLOCK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Remove the front body control module (FBCM) from the fuse box. (See FRONT BODY CONTROL MODULE (FBCM) REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Verify that the voltages of each of the terminals are as indicated in the Terminal Voltage Table (Reference).

  • If the voltage is not as indicated in the table, inspect the parts under Inspection item(s).

    • If the system does not launch normally even though inspection items are normal, specify the location of the malfunction based on the instructions from the on-board diagnostic system and the symptom troubleshooting, and perform repairs.
