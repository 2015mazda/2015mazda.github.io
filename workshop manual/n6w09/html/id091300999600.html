CHILD-RESTRAINT SEAT ANCHOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

Top Tether Anchor

NOTE:

  • When removing the top tether anchor from the vehicle or putting it back in, it can be performed easier by removing the head restraint

1. Open the cover.

2. Remove the bolts.
3. Remove the top tether anchors.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.

ISOFIX Anchor

NOTE:

  • The ISOFIX anchors are integrated into the rear seat hinge.

  • For the ISOFIX anchor removal/installation, refer to the rear seat hinge removal/installation. (See REAR SEAT HINGE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
