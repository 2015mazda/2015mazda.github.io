2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

FRONT BUMPER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

CAUTION:

  • Affix the protective tape to the position (vehicle body side) shown in the figure to prevent scratches and damage.

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove screws A.

3. Remove fasteners B.

4. Bend back the mudguard and then remove screw C.

5. Remove screws D.

6. Remove fasteners E.
7. Slightly bend back front undercover No.1 and remove fasteners F shown in the figure.

8. Remove the fresh-air duct. (See INTAKE-AIR SYSTEM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
9. Remove the set plate. (See SET PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
10. Remove screws G.

11. Remove fasteners H.
12. Grasp the front bumper end, move it in the direction of arrow (1) shown in the figure, and remove the front bumper from the grommet.

13. Pull the edge of the front bumper in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure, then remove tabs I.

CAUTION:

  • The front bumper and front bumper slider are engaged firmly. If they are disengaged forcibly the bumper could fall and be damaged. Perform the servicing carefully when disengaging the front bumper from the front bumper slider.

  • When disengaging the front bumper from the front bumper slider, the front bumper could fall and be damaged. Support the front bumper so that it does not fall.

14. Remove the front bumper from the front bumper slider.
15. Pull the front bumper in the direction of the arrow (3) shown in the figure and remove it while detaching guides J.

16. Disconnect the front fog light connector. (with front fog lights)
17. Disconnect the front ultrasonic sensor connector. (with parking sensor system)
18. Install in the reverse order of removal.
19. Adjust the front fog light aiming. (with front fog lights) (See FRONT FOG LIGHT AIMING.)
