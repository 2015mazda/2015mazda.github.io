ORNAMENT INSTALLATION

CAUTION:

  • Do not allow oil to get on the ornament and the adhesion area of the ornament. Otherwise, the ornament could fall off easily.

1. Degrease the adhesion area of the ornament.
2. Warm the ornament to 15 to 40 degrees using a hot air dryer.
3. Install the ornament to the position shown in the figure.

4. Verify that the clearance between the ornament and the adhesion area of the ornament is 0.8 mm {0.03 in} or less.
