2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

FRONT CENTER SPEAKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the center speaker grille. (See SPEAKER GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Remove the screws.

4. Remove the front center speaker.
5. Disconnect the connector.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
