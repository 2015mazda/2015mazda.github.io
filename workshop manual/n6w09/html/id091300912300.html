2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

REAR SIDE SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Remove the rear seat cushion. (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION)
2. Remove the bolt.

3. Remove the rear side seat back while pulling the hook in the direction of the arrow shown in the figure.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
