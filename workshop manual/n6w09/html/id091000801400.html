2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

HOOD ADJUSTMENT

Height Difference Adjustment

1. Verify that the height difference between the hood and the body is within the specification.

    Standard clearance

  • a: 2.5—6.1 mm {0.10—0.24 in}

  • b: 2.4—4.4 mm {0.10—0.17 in}

2. Loosen bolts and adjust the hood.

3. Tighten bolts. (See HOOD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

Gap Adjustment

1. Verify that the gap between the hood and the body is within the specification.

    Standard clearance

  • a: -2.0—2.0 mm {-0.078—0.078 in}

  • b: -1.0—1.0 mm {-0.039—0.039 in}

2. Turn the stopper rubber to adjust the height of the hood.
