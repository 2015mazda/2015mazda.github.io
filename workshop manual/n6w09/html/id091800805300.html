DISCHARGE HEADLIGHT CONTROL MODULE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

WARNING:

  • Incorrect servicing of the discharge headlights could result in electrical shock. Before servicing the discharge headlights, always refer to the service warnings. (See DISCHARGE HEADLIGHT SERVICE WARNINGS.)

1. Turn the steering wheel to full right lock. (Discharge headlight control module (LH))
2. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
3. Partially peel back the mudguard (LH). (Discharge headlight control module (LH)) (See MUDGUARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
4. Disconnect the discharge headlight control module connector.

5. Rotate the discharge headlight control module in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure to remove it in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure.
6. Rotate the igniter in the direction of the arrow (3) shown in the figure to remove it in the direction of the arrow (4) shown in the figure.

7. Install in the reverse order of removal.
