2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

B-PILLAR UPPER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Remove the following parts:
a. Front scuff plate (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. B-pillar lower trim (See B-PILLAR LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
d. Adjuster anchor cover (See FRONT SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
e. Upper anchor installation bolts on the front seat belt (See FRONT SEAT BELT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Partially peel back the seaming welt.
3. Grasp the shaded area shown in the figure, and pull the B-pillar upper trim in the direction of the arrow (1) while detaching clips A, pin B, pin C.

4. Remove the B-pillar upper trim in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure while detaching the guide D.

CAUTION:

  • If clips A, pin B and pin C are removed forcefully, guide D may be damaged. Carefully remove clips A, pin B and pin C so as not to damage guide D.

5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
