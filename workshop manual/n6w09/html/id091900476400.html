WINDSHIELD WASHER NOZZLE ADJUSTMENT

CAUTION:

  • If the nozzle is adjusted by inserting a stick-like object into the nozzle, it could deform the injection nozzle and the washer fluid may not spray normally. When adjusting the nozzle, do not insert objects into the injection nozzle.

1. Insert a tape-wrapped precision flathead screwdriver into the adjustment hole of the windshield washer nozzle and adjust it to the area where washer fluid is sprayed as shown in the figure.
