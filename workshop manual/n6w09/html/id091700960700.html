2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

SPEAKER GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

Center Speaker Grille

NOTE:

  • When removing the center speaker grille, use the remover tool shown in the figure.

1. To prevent scratches or damage, affix protective tape to the position shown in the figure of the dashboard.

2. To detach clip A from the dashboard, insert the remover tool into the clearance between the center speaker grille and dashboard.
3. Move the remover tool in the direction of arrow (1) shown in the figure and detach clip A from the dashboard.

4. Slide the remover tool to the position shown in the figure with it inserted into the clearance between the center speaker grille and dashboard.

5. Move the remover tool in the direction of arrow (2) shown in the figure to detach clip B from the dashboard.

6. To detach clip C from the dashboard, insert the remover tool into the clearance between the center speaker grille and dashboard.
7. Move the remover tool in the direction of arrow (3) shown in the figure and detach clip C from the dashboard.

8. Slide the remover tool to the position shown in the figure with it inserted into the clearance between the center speaker grille and dashboard.

9. Move the remover tool in the direction of arrow (4) shown in the figure to detach clip D from the dashboard.

10. Hold the shaded area of the center speaker grille, move it in the direction of arrow (5) shown in the figure, detach the hooks from the dashboard, and pull the center speaker grille in the direction of arrow (6) to remove it.

11. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Side Speaker Grille

1. Insert a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver into the service hole and remove hooks A in the direction of arrow (1).

2. Pull the side speaker grille in the direction of the arrow (2), and remove the side speaker grille while detaching hooks B.

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Rear Speaker Grille

1. Insert a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver into shown in the figure and remove clips A in the direction of arrow (1).

2. Pull the rear speaker grille in the direction of the arrow (2), and remove the rear speaker grille while detaching guides B.
3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
