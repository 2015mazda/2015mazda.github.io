HAZARD WARNING SWITCH INSPECTION

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the decoration panel (passenger-side). (See DECORATION PANEL REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Verify that the continuity between the hazard warning switch terminals is as indicated in the table.

  • If the continuity is as indicated in the table, go to the next step.

  • If not as indicated in the table, replace the hazard warning switch. (See HAZARD WARNING SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Apply battery voltage to hazard warning switch terminal A, and connect terminal B to ground.

5. Verify that the LED illuminates.

  • If the LED does not illuminate, replace the hazard warning switch. (See HAZARD WARNING SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
