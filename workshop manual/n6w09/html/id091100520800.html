b. Front scuff plate (See FRONT SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Rear scuff plate (See REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
d. B-pillar lower trim (See B-PILLAR LOWER TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
