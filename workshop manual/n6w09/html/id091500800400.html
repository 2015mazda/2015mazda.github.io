2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

DEFLECTOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Fully open the glass panel.
2. Rotate a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure and remove the spring in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure from hooks A.

CAUTION:

  • If the spring is removed forcefully, hooks A located at the spring installation area may be damaged. Carefully remove the spring so as not to damage hooks A.

3. Remove the deflector in the direction of the arrow in the order of (3), (4) shown in the figure.

4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
