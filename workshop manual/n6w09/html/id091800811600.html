SIDE TURN LIGHT INSPECTION

1. Remove the side turn light. (See SIDE TURN LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Apply battery voltage to side turn light terminal A, and connect terminal B to ground.

3. Verify that the LED illuminates.

  • If the LED illuminates, go to the next step.

  • If the LED does not illuminate, replace the side turn light. (See SIDE TURN LIGHT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

4. Remove the following parts:
a. Inner garnish (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Front door trim (See FRONT DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Power outer mirror connector (See POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
5. Verify that the continuity between side turn light terminal and power outer mirror terminal is as indicated in the table.

  • If not as indicated in the table, replace the power outer mirror. (See POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
