FRONT SIDE SPEAKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

NOTE:

  • If the procedure is performed while holding the front side speaker cone, it could deform the cone causing a malfunction. Therefore, perform the procedure while holding any part other than the cone.

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the side speaker grille. (See SPEAKER GRILLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Remove the screws.

4. Remove the front side speaker.
5. Disconnect the connector.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
