Check code 6


Inspection procedure

Display

Action

Step 1

  • Press the H button to display check code 6.

  • Switch the ignition ON (engine off or on).

  • Verify the screen display.

Go to the next step.

Refer to the clock inspection and verify the terminal voltage at clock terminal A.

(See CLOCK INSPECTION.)

  • Replace the clock if the voltage is B+.

    (See CLOCK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  • Inspect the following parts if the voltage is 1.0 V or less.

    • METER1 10 A fuse

    • IG relay

    • Related wiring harness

Step 2

  • Switch the ignition off (LOCK).

  • Verify the screen display.

Ignition switch ON/OFF signal is normal.

Refer to the clock inspection and verify the terminal voltage at clock terminal A.

(See CLOCK INSPECTION.)

  • Replace the clock if the voltage is 1.0 V or more.

    (See CLOCK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)

  • Inspect the following parts if the voltage is B+.

    • METER1 10 A fuse

    • IG relay

    • Related wiring harness
