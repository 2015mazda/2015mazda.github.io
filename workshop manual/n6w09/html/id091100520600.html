REAR DOOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

WARNING:

  • Removing the rear door without supporting it could cause the rear door to fall and cause serious injury. Always perform the procedure with at least one other person to prevent the rear door from falling.

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove in the order shown in the figure.



1

Bolt A

2

Connector

(See Connector Removal Note.)

3

Bolt B

4

Rear door

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
4. Adjust the rear door. (See DOOR ADJUSTMENT.)

Connector Removal Note

1. While pressing tab A in the direction of arrow (1), disconnect the connector in the direction of arrow (2).
