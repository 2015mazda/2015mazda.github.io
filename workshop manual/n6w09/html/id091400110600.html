2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

LF CONTROL UNIT INSPECTION

1. Remove the service hole cover on the front side trim. (See LF CONTROL UNIT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Verify that the voltages of each of the terminals are as indicated in the terminal voltage table (reference).

  • If the voltage is not as specified in the terminal voltage table (reference), inspect the parts under Inspection item(s).

    • If the system does not work normally even though the inspection items are normal, replace the LF control unit.
