REAR DOOR SPEAKER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

Without Bose®

NOTE:

  • If the procedure is performed while holding the rear door speaker cone, it could deform the cone causing a malfunction. Therefore, perform the procedure while holding any part other than the cone.

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the rear door trim. (See REAR DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Disconnect the connector.

4. Remove the bolts.
5. Pull the rear door speaker in the direction of the arrow shown in the figure and remove it while detaching the pins.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.

With Bose®

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the rear door trim. (See REAR DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Disconnect the connector.

4. Remove the bolts.
5. Pull the rear door speaker in the direction of the arrow shown in the figure and remove it while detaching the pins.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
