REAR SCUFF PLATE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Grasp the shaded area shown in the figure, detach tab A while pulling the rear scuff plate in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure, then detach hook B, clips C, pins D while pulling in the direction of the arrow (2).
2. Grasp the shaded area shown in the figure, detach tab E while pulling the rear scuff plate in the direction of the arrow (3) shown in the figure, then detach the hook F while pulling in the direction of the arrow (4).

3. Install in the reverse order of removal.
