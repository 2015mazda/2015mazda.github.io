POWER OUTER MIRROR INSPECTION

Mirror Glass Adjustment

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the following parts:
a. Inner garnish (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Front door trim (See FRONT DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Power outer mirror (See POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Apply battery positive voltage and connect the ground to the power outer mirror terminals and inspect the power outer mirror operation.

  • If the power outer mirror does not operate as indicated in the table, replace the power outer mirror.

Auto-dimming Outer Mirror Inspection

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the Inner garnish. (See INNER GARNISH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Remove the front door trim. (See FRONT DOOR TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
4. Disconnect the power outer mirror connector.
5. Connect the power outer mirror connector terminal G to a dry cell battery's plus (+) terminal (1.2 V) and terminal E to its minus terminal.

  • Verify that the mirror side of the outer mirror glass changes color.

6. If there is any malfunction, inspect the outer mirror glass. (See OUTER MIRROR GLASS INSPECTION.)

  • If there is no malfunction in the outer mirror glass, replace the power outer mirror. (See POWER OUTER MIRROR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
