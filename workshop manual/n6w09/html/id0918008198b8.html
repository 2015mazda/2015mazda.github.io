FRONT SIDE MARKER/FRONT TURN LIGHT BULB REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [WITH DISCHARGE HEADLIGHT SYSTEM]

1. Turn the steering wheel to full right lock. (Front side marker/Front turn light bulb (LH))
2. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
3. Partially peel back the mudguard (LH). (Front side marker/Front turn light bulb (LH)) (See MUDGUARD REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
4. Rotate the socket in the direction of the arrow (1) shown in the figure and remove it from the front combination light in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure.

5. Remove the front side marker/front turn light bulb.

CAUTION:

  • A bulb generates extremely high heat when it is illuminated. If the surface of the bulb is soiled, excessive heat will build up and the life of the bulb will be shortened. When replacing the bulb, hold the metal flange, not the glass.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
