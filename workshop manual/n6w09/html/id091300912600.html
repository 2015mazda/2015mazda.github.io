REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

CAUTION:

  • When performing the procedure with a rear seat cushion removed from the vehicle, perform the procedure on a clean cloth so as not to damage or soil the seat.

NOTE:

  • When removing the rear seat cushion, perform the procedure in the posture shown in the figure.

1. Move the lever in the direction of arrow (1) shown in the figure, and lift up the rear seat cushion in the direction of arrow (2) shown in the figure while releasing the lock.

2. Fold the rear seat back (RH).
3. Move the hook in the direction of arrow (4) shown in the figure, and set it aside from the center hinge while moving the rear seat cushion in the direction of arrow (3) shown in the figure.

4. Remove the rear seat cushion in the direction of arrow (5) shown in the figure.
5. Install in the reverse order of removal. (See Rear Seat Cushion Installation Note.)

Rear Seat Cushion Installation Note

  • After installing the rear seat cushion, place the rear buckle as shown in the figure.
