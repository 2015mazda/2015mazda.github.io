REAR SEAT HINGE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

Side Hinge

1. Remove the following parts:
a. Trunk covering (See TRUNK COVERING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Rear seat cushion (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
c. Rear side seat back (See REAR SIDE SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
d. Rear seat back (See REAR SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Partially peel back the trunk side trim. (See TRUNK SIDE TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Remove bolt A.

4. Remove bolt B.
5. Remove the side hinge.
6. Install in the reverse order of removal.

Center Hinge

1. Remove the following parts:
a. Rear seat cushion (See REAR SEAT CUSHION REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
b. Rear seat back (See REAR SEAT BACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Remove bolt A.

3. Remove bolt B.
4. Remove the center hinge.
5. Install in the reverse order of removal.
