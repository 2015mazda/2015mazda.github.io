VANITY MIRROR ILLUMINATION BULB REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Insert a tape-wrapped flathead screwdriver into the service hole in the position shown in the figure.

3. While pressing the lens tab in the direction of the arrows (1) shown in the figure, pull the lens in the direction of the arrow (2) shown in the figure to detach the lens tab from the sunvisor.

4. Pull the lens in the direction of the arrow (3) shown in the figure to remove it.
5. Remove the vanity mirror illumination bulb.

6. Install in the reverse order of removal.
