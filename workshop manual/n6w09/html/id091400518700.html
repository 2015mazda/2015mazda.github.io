DOOR LOCK SWITCH INSPECTION

Driver's Side

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the door lock switch. (See POWER WINDOW MAIN SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Verify that the continuity is as indicated in the table.

  • If the continuity is not as indicated in the table, replace the door lock switch.

Passenger's Side

1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)
2. Remove the power window subswitch cover. (See POWER WINDOW SUBSWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
3. Remove the door lock switch. (See DOOR LOCK SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
4. Verify that the continuity is as indicated in the table.

  • If not as indicated in the table, replace the door lock switch.
