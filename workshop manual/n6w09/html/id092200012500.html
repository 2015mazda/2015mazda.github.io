2014 - Mazda6 - Body and Accessories

OIL PRESSURE SWITCH INSPECTION

1. Verify the PCM DTCs, and verify that DTC P0520:00 or P0524:00 is not displayed. (See ON-BOARD DIAGNOSTIC TEST [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • If DTC P0520:00 or P0524:00 is displayed, go to the applicable DTC troubleshooting. (See DTC P0520:00 [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].) (See DTC P0524:00 [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

2. Inspect the oil pressure. (See OIL PRESSURE INSPECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

  • If the oil pressure is normal, replace the oil pressure switch. (See OIL PRESSURE SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
