2014 - Mazda6 - Steering

DTC C200D:1C/C200D:64/U2011:18/U2011:19/U2011:1C/U2011:61/U2011:62/U2011:64/U2011:72



DTC C200D:1C, C200D:64, U2011:1C, U2011:18, U2011:19, U2011:62, U2011:72, U2011:92

EPS motor

DETECTION CONDITION

  • C200D:1C

    • Malfunction detected in resolver sensor internal circuit

  • C200D:64

    • Signal error detected in resolver sensor signal

  • U2011:19, U2011:1C, U2011:18, U2011:61, U2011:62,U2011:64, U2011:72

    • Malfunction detected in EPS motor internal circuit

  • U2011:72

    • When phase 1 open circuit malfunction is detected in the EPS motor, internal circuit current was lower than specification during backup control.

FAIL-SAFE FUNCTION

  • C200D:1C, C200D:64, U2011:18, U2011:19, U2011:1C, U2011:61: U2011:62, U2011:64

    • Control disabled

    • Power steering malfunction indicator light: Illuminated

  • U2011:72

    • Phase 1 open circuit malfunction is detected in the EPS motor

      • Control is maintained in the backup control

    • Other malfunction detected in EPS motor internal circuit

      • Control disabled

    • Power steering malfunction indicator light: Illuminated

POSSIBLE CAUSE

  • Steering column internal malfunction

  • EPS motor malfunction

SYSTEM WIRING DIAGRAM

Not applicable
