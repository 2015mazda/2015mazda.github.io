2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

CLUTCH FLUID INSPECTION [C66M-R]

NOTE:

  • A common reserve tank is used for the clutch system and brake system.

1. Verify that the clutch fluid is between the MIN mark and the MAX mark.

  • If fluid level is lower than MIN mark, perform the following procedure:

    • Verify that there is no fluid leakage. (See CLUTCH FLUID LEAKAGE INSPECTION [C66M-R].)

    • Add fluid to the level between MIN mark and the MAX mark.

    Clutch fluid

  • SAE J1703 or FMVSS116 DOT-3
