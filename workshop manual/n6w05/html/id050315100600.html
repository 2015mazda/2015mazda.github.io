2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

VEHICLE MOVES IN P POSITION, OR PARKING GEAR DOES NOT DISENGAGE WHEN P IS DISENGAGED [FW6A-EL]



TROUBLESHOOTING ITEM

Vehicle moves in P position, or parking gear does not disengage when P is disengaged

DESCRIPTION

  • Vehicle rolls when on a downward slope in P position.

  • Vehicle does not move in D, M and R position when accelerator pedal is depressed, and engine remains in stalled condition.

POSSIBLE CAUSE

  • Parking mechanism malfunction

Diagnostic procedure

STEP

INSPECTION

ACTION

1

 INSPECT PARKING MECHANISM FOR MALFUNCTION

  • When the vehicle is stopped on a flat, level road and the engine is off, does the vehicle move when pushed? (In D, N or R position with the brake pedal released.)

Yes

Symptom troubleshooting is completed.

No

Replace the parking mechanism.

(See AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [FW6A-EL].)
