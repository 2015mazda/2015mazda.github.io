2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

INSPECTION AFTER MANUAL TRANSAXLE OVERHAUL [C66M-R]

1. After warming up the engine, drive the vehicle and inspect the following items:
a. No abnormal noise in each shift position.
b. Smooth shift operation when shifting gears.
c. No gear slipout after shifting gears.
d. Back-up light switch operates correctly.
