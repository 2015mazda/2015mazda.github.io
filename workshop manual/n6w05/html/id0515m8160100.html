2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

OIL SEAL (DIFFERENTIAL) REPLACEMENT [C66M-R]

CAUTION:

  • The sharp edges of the drive shaft can slice or puncture the oil seal. Be careful when installing the drive shaft to the manual transaxle.

1. Remove the front under cover No.2. (See FRONT UNDER COVER No.2 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Drain the manual transaxle oil. (See MANUAL TRANSAXLE OIL REPLACEMENT [C66M-R].)
3. Disconnect the drive shaft (LH) from the manual transaxle. (See FRONT DRIVE SHAFT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
4. Disconnect the drive shaft (RH) from the manual transaxle. (See FRONT DRIVE SHAFT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
5. Remove the oil seal (LH).

6. Remove the oil seal (RH).

7. Using the SSTs (49 U027 003, 49 G030 797) and a hammer, tap a new oil seal (LH) in evenly until the SST (49 U027 003) contacts the transaxle case.

8. Using the SSTs (49 U027 003, 49 G030 797) and a hammer, tap a new oil seal (RH) in evenly until the SST (49 U027 003) contacts the transaxle case.

9. Coat the lip of each oil seal with manual transaxle oil.
10. Install the drive shaft (RH) to the manual transaxle. (See FRONT DRIVE SHAFT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
11. Install the drive shaft (LH) to the manual transaxle. (See FRONT DRIVE SHAFT REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
12. Add the specified amount of specified manual transaxle oil. (See MANUAL TRANSAXLE OIL REPLACEMENT [C66M-R].)
13. Install the front under cover No.2. (See FRONT UNDER COVER No.2 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
