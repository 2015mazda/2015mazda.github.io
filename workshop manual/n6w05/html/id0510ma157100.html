2014 - Mazda6 - Transmission/Transaxle

CLUTCH RELEASE CYLINDER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [C66M-R]

CAUTION:

  • Do not allow clutch fluid to get on a painted surface. Clutch fluid contains properties which can dissolve the paint. If clutch fluid gets on a painted surface, wash it off with water immediately and wipe the area off completely.

1. Remove the front under cover No.2. (See FRONT UNDER COVER No.2 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Disconnect the clutch pipe No.2 from clutch release cylinder, and plug it to avoid clutch fluid leakage. (See CLUTCH PIPE AND HOSE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [C66M-R].)
3. Remove the clutch release cylinder.

4. Install the clutch release cylinder.

5. Connect the clutch pipe No.2 to clutch release cylinder. (See CLUTCH PIPE AND HOSE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [C66M-R].)
6. Install the front under cover No.2. (See FRONT UNDER COVER No.2 REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
7. Bleed the air from the clutch system. (See CLUTCH FLUID REPLACEMENT/AIR BLEEDING [C66M-R].)
