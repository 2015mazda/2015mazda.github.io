2014 - Mazda6 - DriveLine/Axle

WHEEL HUB, STEERING KNUCKLE INSPECTION

Wheel Bearing Excessive Play Inspection

1. Remove the brake caliper component and disc plate. (See WHEEL HUB, STEERING KNUCKLE REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)
2. Install the magnetic base and dial gauge as shown in the figure and measure the wheel bearing axial excessive play.

  • If it exceeds the maximum specification, replace the wheel hub bearing.

    Front wheel bearing maximum play

  • 0.05 mm {0.002 in}
