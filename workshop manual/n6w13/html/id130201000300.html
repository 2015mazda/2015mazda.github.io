2014 - Mazda6 - I-ELOOP

CLEARING DTC [i-ELOOP]

1. Connect the M-MDS to the DLC–2.
2. After the vehicle is identified, select the following items from the initialization screen of the M-MDS.
a. Select “Self Test”.
b. Select “Modules”.
c. Select “DCDC”.
3. Then, select the “Retrieve CMDTCs” and perform procedures according to directions on the M-MDS screen.
4. Verify the DTC according to the directions on the M-MDS screen.
5. Press the clear button on the DTC screen to clear the DTC.
6. Verify that no DTCs are displayed.
7. Switch the ignition off.
