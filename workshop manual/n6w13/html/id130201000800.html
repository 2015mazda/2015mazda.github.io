DTC P0C39:00 [i-ELOOP]



DTC P0C39:00

Capacitor (i-ELOOP) temperature sensor system: function/circuit malfunction

DETECTION CONDITION

  • Any one of the following conditions is met:

    • A condition in which the capacitor (i-ELOOP) temperature sensor voltage is less than 0.05 V (less than −50 °C {-58°F}) continues for 1 s.

    • A condition in which the capacitor (i-ELOOP) temperature sensor voltage is 4.95 V or more (90 °C {194°F} or more) continues for 1 s.

FAIL-SAFE FUNCTION

  • Not applicable

POSSIBLE CAUSE

  • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) connector or terminals malfunction

  • DC-DC converter connector or terminals malfunction

  • Short to ground in wiring harness between capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1A and DC-DC converter terminal 1E

  • Short to power supply in wiring harness between capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1A and DC-DC converter terminal 1E

  • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) temperature sensor signal circuit and ground circuit are shorted to each other

  • Open circuit in wiring harness between the following terminals:

    • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1A—DC-DC converter terminal 1E

    • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1B—DC-DC converter terminal 1D

  • Capacitor (i-ELOOP) temperature sensor malfunction

  • DC-DC converter malfunction

4

 INSPECT CAPACITOR (i-ELOOP) TEMPERATURE SENSOR SIGNAL CIRCUIT FOR SHORT TO GROUND

  • Verify that the capacitor (i-ELOOP) and DC-DC converter connectors are disconnected.

  • Inspect for continuity between capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1A (wiring harness-side) and body ground.

  • Is there continuity?

Yes

Refer to the wiring diagram and verify whether or not there is a common connector between capacitor (i-ELOOP) terminal 1A and DC-DC converter terminal 1E.

    If there is a common connector:

  • Determine the malfunctioning part by inspecting the common connector and the terminal for corrosion, damage, or pin disconnection, and the common wiring harness for a short to ground.

  • Repair or replace the malfunctioning part.

    If there is no common connector:

  • Repair or replace the wiring harness which has a short to ground.

Go to Step 9.

Go to the next step.

9

 VERIFY DTC TROUBLESHOOTING COMPLETED

    (See NEGATIVE BATTERY CABLE DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [SKYACTIV-G 2.5].)

    (See SERVICE PLUG DISCONNECTION/CONNECTION [i-ELOOP].)

    (See CLEARING DTC [i-ELOOP].)

  • Retrieve the DC-DC converter DTCs using the M-MDS.

    (See DTC INSPECTION [i-ELOOP].)

  • Is the same DTC present?

Yes

Repeat the inspection from Step 1.

  • If the malfunction recurs, replace the DC-DC converter.

    (See DC-DC CONVERTER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION [i-ELOOP].)

Go to the next step.

No

Go to the next step.

10

(See DTC TABLE [i-ELOOP].)

No
